Theme: Industry 4.0 and Machine Learning

Weekly focus

The final unit moved from individual methods to the wider industrial context in which machine learning is deployed. Topics such as digital twins, edge AI and the future of work helped connect technical learning back to organisational and social change.

Commentary on learning

This was a useful end point because it placed the module in a wider professional context. It reinforced the idea that machine learning professionals need not only technical competence, but also the ability to judge relevance, risk and long-term impact.
