Weekly focus

Although this unit focused on NLP and transformer-based developments, it also highlighted how quickly the field moves beyond the foundational techniques covered earlier in the module.

Commentary on learning

The value of the unit was in showing that modern machine learning is broader than individual algorithms. It also reinforced the role of evaluation metrics and the need to understand why a method is appropriate in a given context, rather than being driven only by what is currently fashionable.
