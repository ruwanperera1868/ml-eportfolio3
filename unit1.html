Theme: Introduction to Machine Learning

Weekly focus

The first unit introduced machine learning as a field and situated it within broader developments such as big data, artificial intelligence and Industry 4.0. My main focus this week was understanding the categories of machine learning, the role of machine learning in industry and the skills required to work effectively in this area.

Commentary on learning

The collaborative discussion on the Fourth Industrial Revolution was especially useful because it pushed me to connect machine learning to real sectors rather than thinking about it in abstract terms. It also introduced the legal, social and professional dimension of the module from the beginning.
