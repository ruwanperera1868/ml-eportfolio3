Theme: Correlation and Regression

Weekly focus

This unit developed the theory behind correlation and regression. Running the supplied notebooks helped me see how coefficient values and data structure affect the relationship between variables and the fitted line.

Commentary on learning

The practical value of this unit was in moving from formula-based understanding to interpretation. I became more aware that correlation is useful for describing association, but regression becomes more meaningful when the model assumptions and the purpose of prediction are made explicit.
