Unit: 11
Theme: Model Selection and Evaluation

Weekly focus

This unit drew together performance measurement, hyperparameter tuning and the broader idea of model operations. It mattered because it shifted attention from building models to judging whether they are trustworthy, reproducible and suitable for deployment.

Commentary on learning

That shift was important for my own thinking. It encouraged me to treat evaluation as a process of decision-making rather than a final technical check. It also linked directly to the final project work, where metrics had to be interpreted alongside limitations.
