Unit: 7
Theme: Introduction to Artificial Neural Networks

Weekly focus

The neural network unit introduced the perceptron, activation functions and the logic of layered learning. This was a clear increase in conceptual complexity compared with regression and clustering.

Commentary on learning

The main value of the unit was showing that neural networks should not be treated as black boxes. Understanding the perceptron and the role of activation functions helped reduce some of the mystery around how networks produce outputs.
