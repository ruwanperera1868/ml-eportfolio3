Unit: 8
Theme: Training an Artificial Neural Network

Weekly focus

This unit focused on backpropagation, gradient descent and the way networks learn from error. The practical gradient descent activity was particularly useful because it showed how learning rate and iteration choices affect convergence.

Commentary on learning

The main learning point here was that neural training is sensitive to parameter choices. This made model tuning feel less abstract and highlighted why deep learning results must be interpreted as the product of many design decisions rather than a single automated process.
