Unit: 9
Theme: Introduction to Convolutional Neural Networks

Weekly focus

This unit extended neural learning into computer vision. It introduced CNNs and the wider applications of image-based machine learning, while also asking us to think about ethical and social implications.

Commentary on learning

What stood out for me was that performance and risk increase together. CNNs are powerful, but the potential for surveillance misuse, bias and over-confidence in automated vision systems means that technical work has to be accompanied by ethical judgement.
