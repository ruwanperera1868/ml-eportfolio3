Unit 6 Project Proposal

The proposal stage focused on defining the business question, identifying the dataset and selecting methods that were realistic for the project timescale. The planned project aimed to analyse Airbnb pricing in New York City and to explore whether machine learning could support pricing optimisation and market segmentation.

At this stage, the strongest ideas were the use of regression for interpretable price modelling and clustering for segment discovery. The proposal was sound, but it was naturally more tentative than the final project because some assumptions about feature usefulness and model suitability still had to be tested through implementation.

Planned contribution

My expected contribution at proposal stage was to support the analytical design of the work, contribute to the choice and interpretation of methods, and help ensure that the final output was understandable to a non-technical reader. In hindsight, the proposal also showed that I was still thinking in a fairly model-centric way. I was more focused on what methods might be used than on what assumptions would later need to be challenged.
